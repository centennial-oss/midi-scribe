# MIDI Scribe Privacy Policy

## Summary

MIDI Scribe does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** MIDI Scribe does not:

- Collect personal information
- Transmit data over the internet
- Use analytics or tracking
- Communicate with any external servers

The app has no network entitlements and cannot make or accept network connections.

## Data Storage

App data never leaves your private storage: either locally on your Mac, or in your iCloud account if you turn on iCloud sync. This app cannot write to iCloud, it writes to your local iCloud drive, which your device's Operating System syncs to your account in the background.

## Changes

We may update this policy occasionally, but will keep a concise log of all changes. We will never update this policy to permit snooping or analytics collection.
